# Retrieval Is Context-Blind
*â–³ problem*

Grep, full-text search, and semantic search are excellent at finding text. But they do not answer: What decision did we ultimately make? What is still unresolved? Which ideas were rejected? Which reasoning is current vs obsolete? Search retrieves data. It does not govern meaning.

## Why it matters

As knowledge bases grow, the gap between "finding text" and "understanding context" becomes critical. More search results actually make the problem worse without governance.
